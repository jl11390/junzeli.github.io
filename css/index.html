Bachelor of Science in Risk Management and Business Intelligence

The RMBI program is the first undergraduate degree program in Hong Kong to integrate risk management and business intelligence. It opens up a unique opportunity for talented students with a strong background in quantitative and computing skills to empower themselves to meet the challenges of these sought-after professional fields.

Through in-depth study of an actual business case, students strengthen their ability to apply knowledge from different areas in real-world situations, develop their creativity and critical thinking, and improve their project management skills.
